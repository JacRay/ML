Suppose you want to marry 1000 men and 1000 women
Given a set of men X
and set of women W 
Each men ranks women, and each women ranks the men

Solution:
men propose their highest ranked women who they have not got rejected
women accepts their highest ranked men even if they are already married to a lower ranked man

Example: if Carl and David both prefer Alice to Barbara and
Alice and Barbara both prefer Carl to David

First Carl and David propose Alice(Their first preference) ,
Alice Marry Carl(Her First preference)
David propose Barbara(His next preference who did not reject him)
Barbara Marry David?(Her only option)

Some Applications:
*Matching residents to Hospitals in US
*National university entrance exam in iran
*Placement of Canadian Lawers
*Sorority Rush
